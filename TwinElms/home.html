Twin Elms Environmental Brokerage is exclusively dedicated to the placement of environmental insurance.

We specialize in helping clients throughout the United States to manage the risks and liabilities associated with the ownership of environmentally impaired properties. Twin Elms works with all major environmental insurance carriers crafting customized policies that provide clients with the most complete coverage available, addressing the unique challenges associated their property.

Advanced Risk Management has replaced cost prohibitive risk elimination as the primary means for addressing the potential liabilities associated with environmentally impacted properties. Insurance can help unlock the embedded value of properties that may have been unfit for development just a few years ago.

Environmental Insurance is routinely used in commercial real estate transactions to assist buyers, sellers, lenders and associated parties offset existing liabilities. It has also enabled refinancing that would have previously stalled or failed due to concerns associated with environmental liability.

Our practice is a demonstrated leader in this highly specialized field. We take great pride in our unparalleled reputation for innovation, efficiency and attention to detail in providing our expertise. With over eleven years of dedicated experience in the environmental insurance marketplace, we believe that we are uniquely qualified to support your environmental risk management.

When facing the unique challenges of owning, operating real estate with known or potential environmental exposures, or, if you are considering the acquisition, sale or redevelopment of a property with environmental concerns, Twin Elms can deliver insurance solutions that help manage environmental risk and secure the value of your assets.

We look forward to an opportunity to work with you on your next project. Please feel free to contact us with any questions, or for further information.
